JCHAT User Guide

Welcome to the JCHAT User Guide! This guide will help you understand all the features of JCHAT and how to make the most of your experience. Whether you're new or a seasoned user, you'll find valuable information here.

1. Introduction to JCHAT

JCHAT is a dynamic social platform designed to connect people through posts, chats, and a unique JCoin economy. Share your thoughts, connect with friends, and explore a community that values interaction and engagement.

2. Getting Started

Account Creation & Login

To begin your JCHAT journey, you'll need to create an account. Simply follow the on-screen prompts during registration. Once registered, you can log in using your credentials. If you're new, you might be signed in anonymously first, but we encourage you to create a full account to unlock all features.

  • Register: Provide a username, email, and password.
  • Login: Use your registered email and password to access your account.

3. Home Page

The Home Page is your central hub for daily updates and interactions.

Daily Inspiration

Start your day with a dose of positivity! The Daily Inspiration section provides quotes tailored to your preferences. You can:

  • Share / Post: Share the current inspiration directly to your feed.
  • Customize Inspiration: Choose your preferred type of inspiration (motivational, peaceful, funny, wisdom).
  • Claim Daily Bonus: Log in daily to claim JCoins and XP!

Create New Post

Share your thoughts, photos, or videos with the JCHAT community.

  • Text Content: Write what's on your mind.
  • Add Media: Upload images or videos from your device.
  • Post: Publish your content to your feed.

Posts Feed & Interactions

See posts from other users and interact with them.

  • React: Express yourself with 'Like' , 'Love' , or 'Haha' reactions.
  • Comment: Share your thoughts and engage in discussions.
  • Edit/Delete Posts: Manage your own posts using the options menu .
  • Share Posts: Share interesting posts with others.

4. Chat

Connect with your friends and other users in real-time.

Sending Messages

  • Text Messages: Type and send instant messages.
  • Attach Media: Share images or audio files using the paperclip icon .
  • Voice Notes: Record and send voice messages using the microphone icon .
  • Read Receipts: See when your messages have been read (double checkmark ).
  • Typing Indicator: See when your chat partner is typing.

Voice & Video Calls (Simulated)

Initiate simulated voice or video calls with your chat partners. Note: These are simulated calls for demonstration purposes and do not connect to actual phone lines or video streams.

Message Options

Right-click or long-press on a message to access a context menu:

  • Edit: Modify your sent text messages.
  • Delete: Remove your messages from the chat.
  • Star/Unstar: Mark important messages for easy retrieval later.

Search Messages

Use the search icon in the chat header to find specific messages within your conversations.

5. Profile

Your personal space on JCHAT.

  • View Profile: See your profile picture, username, bio, and other details.
  • Edit Profile: Update your profile picture, username, bio, and location.
  • Privacy Settings: Control the visibility of your profile information.
  • XP & Levels: Track your experience points and progression through JCHAT levels.
  • JCoins: View your JCoin balance, the in-app currency.

6. Friends

Connect with other users on JCHAT.

  • Find Users: Search for other JCHAT users.
  • Send/Accept Requests: Send friend requests and manage incoming requests.
  • Friends List: View your current friends and their online status.

7. Groups

Join or create groups to chat with multiple people about shared interests.

  • Create Groups: Start your own community.
  • Join Groups: Discover and join existing groups.
  • Group Chat: Communicate with all members of a group.

8. JCoin Shop

Spend your earned JCoins on exciting in-app items and features.

  • Browse Items: Explore available items in the shop.
  • Purchase: Use your JCoins to acquire items.
  • Unlock Wallet: Access advanced JCoin features (requires admin approval).

9. Level Benefits & Progression System

JCHAT features a comprehensive 500+ level progression system designed to reward your engagement and provide exciting goals. Each level brings unique benefits and unlocks new opportunities in your JCHAT journey.

How to Level Up

Earn XP (Gas) through various activities:

  • Messaging: 1-5 XP per message sent
  • Group Participation: 2-8 XP per group interaction
  • Social Activity: 3-10 XP for friend connections
  • Daily Login: 5-15 XP bonus
  • Community Tasks: 10-50 XP per task
  • Achievements: 25-100 XP per milestone

Level Tier Rewards

Levels 1-50: Newcomer

  • JCoins: 10-50 JCoins per level
  • Badges: Basic badges & titles
  • Themes: 3 profile themes available
  • Shop Discount: 5% discount on all items

Levels 51-150: Explorer

  • JCoins: 51-100 JCoins per level
  • Badges: Advanced badges & titles
  • Themes: 8 profile themes available
  • Shop Discount: 10% discount on all items
  • Access: VIP groups and exclusive content

Levels 151-300: Champion

  • JCoins: 101-200 JCoins per level
  • Badges: Exclusive premium badges
  • Themes: 15 profile themes available
  • Shop Discount: 15% discount on all items
  • Priority: Priority message delivery
  • Tools: Enhanced moderation tools

Levels 301-500: Master

  • JCoins: 201-350 JCoins per level
  • Badges: Legendary badges & titles
  • Themes: Unlimited profile themes
  • Shop Discount: 25% discount on all items
  • Support: VIP customer support
  • Voice Chat: Voice chat privileges
  • Customization: Advanced customization options

Levels 500+: Legend

  • JCoins: 400+ JCoins per level
  • Badges: Mythical badges & titles
  • Themes: Custom theme creation abilities
  • Shop Discount: 35% discount on all items
  • Moderation: Community moderation privileges
  • Leadership: Community leadership roles
  • Beta Access: Early access to new features
  • Recognition: Hall of Fame recognition

Maximizing Your Benefits

Daily Strategies

  • Daily Login: Log in every day for bonus XP
  • Active Participation: Engage in group chats and discussions
  • Social Connections: Connect with new friends regularly
  • Daily Challenges: Complete daily tasks and challenges

Long-term Goals

  • Milestone Levels: Aim for key levels (50, 100, 250, 500)
  • Badge Collection: Collect badges from all categories
  • Leadership Roles: Work towards unlocking community leadership
  • Legend Status: Reach the prestigious 500+ level range

Pro Tip: Consistent daily activity is more valuable than sporadic bursts - steady progress unlocks the best rewards!

10. Settings

Personalize your JCHAT experience.

  • Theme Customization: Change the look and feel of your app (Light, Dark, Glass, Sunset modes).
  • Notification Settings: (Future feature) Manage how you receive alerts.
  • Privacy Settings: Control who sees your profile information.

11. Solution Center

Find answers to common questions and get support.

  • FAQs: Browse frequently asked questions.
  • Contact Support: Reach out to the JCHAT support team for assistance.

12. Admin Features

These features are exclusively for JCHAT administrators to manage the platform.

  • Admin Dashboard: Overview of system status and key metrics.
  • Coin Management: Manage JCoins and XP for users.
  • User Management: Oversee user accounts, including banning and unbanning.
  • System Settings: Control global application settings like maintenance mode and feature toggles.
  • Visitor Tracker: Monitor website visitor activity.

13. Tips & Security

Keep your JCHAT experience safe and enjoyable.

  • Password Security: Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Privacy: Be mindful of what you share and adjust your privacy settings.
  • Reporting: Report any inappropriate content or behavior to the JCHAT team.
  • Internet Connection: Ensure a stable internet connection for the best experience.
