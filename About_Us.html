Level Benefits & Progression System

JCHAT features a comprehensive 500+ level progression system designed to reward your engagement and provide exciting goals. Each level brings unique benefits and unlocks new opportunities in your JCHAT journey.

How to Level Up

Earn XP (Gas) through various activities:

  • Messaging: 1-5 XP per message sent
  • Group Participation: 2-8 XP per group interaction
  • Social Activity: 3-10 XP for friend connections
  • Daily Login: 5-15 XP bonus
  • Community Tasks: 10-50 XP per task
  • Achievements: 25-100 XP per milestone
